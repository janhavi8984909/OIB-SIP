# Data Dictionary

## Customer Demographics
- `Income`: Customer's yearly household income
- `Kidhome`: Number of small children in customer's household
- `Teenhome`: Number of teenagers in customer's household
- `Customer_Days`: Number of days since customer registration

## Education Level
- `education_2n Cycle`: Secondary education
- `education_Basic`: Basic education
- `education_Graduation`: Bachelor's degree
- `education_Master`: Master's degree
- `education_PhD`: PhD degree

## Marital Status
- `marital_Divorced`: Divorced
- `marital_Married`: Married
- `marital_Single`: Single
- `marital_Together`: In relationship
- `marital_Widow`: Widow/Widower

## Product Spending
- `MntWines`: Amount spent on wine products
- `MntFruits`: Amount spent on fruit products
- `MntMeatProducts`: Amount spent on meat products
- `MntFishProducts`: Amount spent on fish products
- `MntSweetProducts`: Amount spent on sweet products
- `MntGoldProds`: Amount spent on gold products
- `MntTotal`: Total amount spent on all products

## Purchase Channels
- `NumDealsPurchases`: Purchases made with discount
- `NumWebPurchases`: Purchases through company's website
- `NumCatalogPurchases`: Purchases using catalogue
- `NumStorePurchases`: Purchases directly in stores
